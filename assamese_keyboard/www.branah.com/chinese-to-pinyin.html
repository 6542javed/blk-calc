Chinese to Pinyin Converter enables you to easily convert text containing Chinese characters (Hanzi) to Pinyin. The converter only replaces the Hanzi found in the input text. The Hanzi database used contains about 41,000 characters.

In cases where a Hanzi has more than one reading, the readings will be shown in brackets. For example, the Hanzi conversion for 不 will be shown as [bu4 bu5].
