Cantonese to Jyutping Converter enables you to easily convert Cantonese to Jyutping. The converter only replaces the Chinese characters found in the input text. There are about 23,000 characters in the database.

In cases where a character has more than one reading, the readings will be shown in brackets. For example, the conversion for 語 will be shown as [ jyu5 jyu6].
